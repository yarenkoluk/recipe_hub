Broccoli Salad

A fresh and healthy salad made with broccoli, cherry tomatoes, and a lemon vinaigrette.

For 2-4 people, 10 minutes preparation, 5 minutes cooking

Nutritional Value
Calories 257
Carbohydrates 16.40g
Protein 6.70g
Fat 19.40g

Materials

  • 1 head of broccoli
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

The Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Wash and chop the broccoli into florets.
  3. Place the broccoli on a baking sheet and drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until tender.
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice and olive oil.
  7. Combine the roasted broccoli with cherry tomatoes in a large bowl.
  8. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
Chef's Note: Add some sunflower seeds for a delicious crunch!

Baked Potatoes

Crispy and healthy oven-baked potato wedges.

For 2-4 people, 15 minutes preparation, 25 minutes cooking

Nutritional Value
Calories 150
Carbohydrates 30g
Protein 3g
Fat 5g

Materials

  • 3 medium potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon thyme

The Recipe

  1. Slice the potatoes into wedges.
  2. Mix with olive oil and spices.
  3. Bake at 200°C for 25 minutes.
